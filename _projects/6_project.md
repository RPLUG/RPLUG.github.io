## Industrial context

This Baosteel research project modelled charging and burden distribution in the upper part of a blast furnace. The work connected charging strategy with burden trajectory, surface formation, and the resulting material distribution.

### Contributions

- Constructed particle-scale charging and burden-distribution models.
- Analysed burden trajectory and surface profile under different charging strategies.
- Quantified ore-to-coke ratio and particle-size distribution.
- Supported operating optimization and coke-consumption reduction.

**Project period:** September 2019 - November 2021

**Industrial partner:** Baosteel
