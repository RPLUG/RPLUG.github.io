## Industrial context

This Baosteel research project investigated the discharge behaviour of a rotary storage device used for blast-furnace slag. A particle-flow model was developed to quantify the effects of operating conditions and internal geometry.

### Contributions

- Compared feeding modes and storage volumes.
- Quantified the effects of rotation speed and lifter geometry on discharge rate.
- Evaluated discharge stability and supported equipment optimization.
- Translated simulation results into operating and structural recommendations.

**Project period:** May 2024 - May 2026

**Industrial partner:** Baosteel
